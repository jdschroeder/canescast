Get Your First Look at the 2013-2014 Hurricanes

August 28, 2013 - by Josh Schroeder

The vets and draft picks have already been on the ice, and tonight is the eve of training camp officially opening for the Lethbridge Hurricanes, and that means it’s just about time time to get your first look at this year’s team. Here’s the schedule for training camp and the pre-season.

Training Camp

Training camp sessions take place at the Enmax Centre and admission is free.

Friday, August 30th - Scrimmages from 9:00 AM until 4:45 PM
Saturday, August 31st - Scrimmages from 9:00 AM until 6:00 PM
Sunday, September 1st - Scrimmages from 9:00 AM until 1:15 PM

Pre-season games

Thursday, September 5th, 7:00 at the Taber Arena vs. Calgary

Tickets are $10 and available at Joe’s Skate Sharpening, Health Connection, or at the door of the Taber arena (4716- 50th St.)

Wednesday, September 11th, 7:00 at the Enmax Centre vs. Medicine Hat

Tickets are $10 and available from Lethbridge Minor Hockey Association Office (Upstairs of Adams Arena, 1302 - 9th Avenue North, Phone: 403-320-0745) or before the game CASH ONLY (NW Entrance doors).

Friday, September 13th, 7:00 in the Crowsnest Pass vs. Kootenay
Saturday, September 14th 7:00 in the Crowsnest Pass vs. Calgary

Admission is free and on a first come, first served basis. For more information on the games in the Crowsnest Pass, contact Chad Petrone of Crowsnest Minor Hockey via email at: chad@logomotives.ca.

Josh Schroeder

Josh’s hockey life has been intertwined with the Hurricanes since he took to the ice for training camp in 1999. Since then, he has spent countless hours in the Enmax Centre, occupying seats in section OO, the press box, and that tiny space between the players’ benches. In 2011 he designed the Hurricanes’ popular Hawker Hurricane third jersey emblem.

As a member of the CanesCast team, Josh builds and maintains CanesCast.ca, writes articles, and shoots game photos.
