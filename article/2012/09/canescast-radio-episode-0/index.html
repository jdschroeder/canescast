CanesCast Radio - Episode 0

September 13, 2012 - by Josh Schroeder

A new season is upon us, and we at CanesCast have some great stuff in store for the year. The first thing to unveil is our new podcast CanesCast Radio. We have dubbed our first recording "episode 0", sort of a pre-season for us. So without further ado, here is episode 0, recorded on location at the Enmax Centre prior to the Hurricanes' win over the Tigers last night.

We'll soon have the episodes available for subscription in iTunes, or other podcast software. We hope you'll enjoy it.

Josh Schroeder

Josh’s hockey life has been intertwined with the Hurricanes since he took to the ice for training camp in 1999. Since then, he has spent countless hours in the Enmax Centre, occupying seats in section OO, the press box, and that tiny space between the players’ benches. In 2011 he designed the Hurricanes’ popular Hawker Hurricane third jersey emblem.

As a member of the CanesCast team, Josh builds and maintains CanesCast.ca, writes articles, and shoots game photos.
