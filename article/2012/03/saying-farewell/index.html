Saying Farewell

March 11, 2012 - by Morgan Martin

Tonights game is the final Hurricanes home game for the 2011 - 2012 season. The night started with the 2012 Hurricanes Awards Ceremony (see the list of winners here) and lead up to the final home games as Hurricanes for both Damien Ketlo and Brody Sutter. Tonight the Tigers rolled into town and the Hurricanes looked to go out with a strong final showing for the locals.

What happened

The first period got off to a rough start as the Tigers scored early and racked up a 2 goal lead midway through. Inspite of some decent chances the Canes weren't able to put themselves on the scoreboard.

The Canes picked up the play in the second period as Bezuch was able to snipe one in before we hit the third minute. The Canes play in this period was more inspired then the first and they came out up one goal and down only by 4 in shots. One event of note was a crushing hit by Kessey followed by another hit on Jayden Hart by Kizuik which resulted in a fight between Kizuik and Hurley. Large penalties including a game misconduct to Kessey were one result. More significantly a lengthy delay ensued as Hart had to be stretchered off the ice.

Back and forth play was the hallmark of the third period. Chances and strong goaltending on both sides of the ice made the first half of the period exciting hockey to watch. Just before the halfway mark of the period there was some drama around the hurricanes net. The puck looked like it may have crossed, the ref was pointing but the puck squirted free. The call on the review was no goal. THe final minutes ticked away with the hurricanes struggling to make up the goal and send this into overtime. Tot rang one off the post, but that was all they can do giving up one final empty goal in the last .8 seconds, losing the home final 3 - 1.
