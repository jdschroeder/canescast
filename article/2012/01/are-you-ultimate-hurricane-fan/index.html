Are you the Ultimate Hurricane Fan?

January 30, 2012 - by Josh Schroeder

If you think you are the ultimate Lethbridge Hurricane fan and are prepared to prove it, you could earn yourself a trip to the 2012 Memorial Cup in Shawinigan. Here’s how it works:

  1. Get dressed up in your best Hurricane garb. Jerseys, costumes, face paint, you name it. Go all out!
  2. Show up at BMO downtown (604 – 4th Ave. South) tomorrow (Tuesday, January 31) by 5:30. The first 100 registered on-site will be eligible to participate.
  3. A panel of judges including captain Brody Sutter and game host Brad Lang will pick the top three fans.
  4. The top three will have their knowledge of the Hurricanes tested with a series of trivia questions.
  5. The winner will receive a special team jersey and a 1 in 15 chance of winning a trip to the 2012 Memorial Cup.

Easy as that. Come on out and show us how big a Hurricane fan you are!

Josh Schroeder

Josh’s hockey life has been intertwined with the Hurricanes since he took to the ice for training camp in 1999. Since then, he has spent countless hours in the Enmax Centre, occupying seats in section OO, the press box, and that tiny space between the players’ benches. In 2011 he designed the Hurricanes’ popular Hawker Hurricane third jersey emblem.

As a member of the CanesCast team, Josh builds and maintains CanesCast.ca, writes articles, and shoots game photos.
