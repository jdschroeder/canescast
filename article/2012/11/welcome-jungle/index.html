November 02, 2012 - by Morgan Martin

It's been a while in the coming, but Axel Blomqvist potted his first this season on Tuesday. In spite of some solid play, Axel has been a bit snake bit and it's fantastic to see him put one (and a nice one at that) behind the opposing goaltender. Maxwell

Continuing his story this season Russ added three more points on the night. On pace for about 50 goals this season, Russ is a cornerstone of the offence this year and always a threat.

OT Misery

Inspite of a late lead and taking a power play into the OT period, the Canes weren't able to get that final goal. Shut down by former Cane Liam Liston in the shootout the canes were beat on the final shot of the first round. Both Dan Johnston and Axel felt that the team needs to be more consistent in the first period.

The good

Despite the loss the Canes play from the second on was solid. If an unlucky pinball shot had not of found the back of the net late in the third this was a different game. They never seem out of the game currently, and it always feels like they have enough in the tank to take the game whatever might happen early on. A little more consistency in the early parts of the game and they will be a threat to any team in the league.
