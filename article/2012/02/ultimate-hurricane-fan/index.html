The Ultimate Hurricane Fan

February 01, 2012 - by Josh Schroeder

Our congratulations to Karren Neufeld, the Hurricanes’ Ultimate Fan as decided yesterday at BMO. Looking the part in her Hurricanes jersey, Karren showed plenty of personality and breezed through qualifying to win her spot in the trivia round. It took a few rounds to break the stalemate, but eventually her Hurricane knowledge won out as well to earn her the autographed jersey, chance for a trip to the Memorial Cup, and title of Ultimate Fan.

Karren Neufeld poses with Landon Oslanski, Graham Hood, Brody Sutter, and Brady Ramsay

Good luck in the draw for the trip, Karren. We hope you’ll get to represent Lethbridge in Shawinigan later this year.

Josh Schroeder

Josh’s hockey life has been intertwined with the Hurricanes since he took to the ice for training camp in 1999. Since then, he has spent countless hours in the Enmax Centre, occupying seats in section OO, the press box, and that tiny space between the players’ benches. In 2011 he designed the Hurricanes’ popular Hawker Hurricane third jersey emblem.

As a member of the CanesCast team, Josh builds and maintains CanesCast.ca, writes articles, and shoots game photos.
