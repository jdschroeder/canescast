Heritage Night and Sugar Kings jerseys

January 18, 2012 - Mark your calendars for Friday, January 27, as the Hurricanes’ 25th anniversary celebrations will continue in their game against the Medicine Hat Tigers. The ‘Canes will sport throw-back uniforms for “Heritage Night”, wearing Lethbridge Sugar Kings colours and welcoming former Sugar King (and Tiger, as a matter of fact) and Hockey Hall of Famer Lanny McDonald to the Enmax Centre. After starring with the Sugar Kings and Tigers, McDonald went on to a successful 16 year NHL career with the Toronto Maple Leafs, Colorado Rockies, and Calgary Flames, scoring 500 goals along the way and capping his career with a Stanley Cup championship in 1989.
